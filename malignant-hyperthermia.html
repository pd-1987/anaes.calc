Malignant Hyperthermia
Dantrolene

Immediate treatment

Bolus (2-3 mg/kg): 0 mg

Maximum dose (10mg/kg): 0 mg

Every 5 minutes, until ETCO2 <6 kPa and temperature <38.5C

Bolus (1 mg/kg): 0 mg

Pause and observe

Repeat 1 mg.kg-1 to maintain ETCO2 <6 kPa and temp <38.5C, even if it exceeds ‘maximum’ dose 10 mg/kg
