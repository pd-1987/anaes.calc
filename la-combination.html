Local Anaesthetic Combination Calculator

Use ideal body weight if BMI ≥ 35 kg/m²

Bupivacaine / Levobupivacaine: A maximum dose of 2 mg/kg should not be exceeded in any four-hour period; Bupivacaine not to exceed 175 mg per dose, Levobupivacaine not to exceed 200 mg per dose. In total ≤ 400 mg/24 h.

Ropivacaine: A maximum dose of 3 mg/kg should not be exceeded in any four- to six-hour period, not to exceed 200 mg per dose. In total ≤ 675 mg/24h.
