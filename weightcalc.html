Ideal Body Weight (IBW) – Devine formula: For males, 50 + 0.9055 × (height in cm – 152.4); for females, 45.5 + 0.9055 × (height in cm – 152.4). Note: Formula not validated for heights below 152.4 cm/5 ft.

Adjusted Body Weight: IBW + 0.4 × (Actual weight – IBW).

Lean Body Weight – Boer formula: For males, 0.407 × weight + 0.267 × height in cm – 19.2; for females, 0.252 × weight + 0.473 × height in cm – 48.3.
