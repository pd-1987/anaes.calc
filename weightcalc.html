Formulas used: Ideal Body Weight (IBW) – Devine formula: For males, IBW = 50 + 0.91 × (height in cm – 152.4); for females, IBW = 45.5 + 0.91 × (height in cm – 152.4). Adjusted Body Weight (ABW) = IBW + 0.4 × (Actual weight – IBW). Lean Body Weight (LBW) – Boer formula: For males, LBW = 0.407 × weight + 0.267 × height in cm – 19.2; for females, LBW = 0.252 × weight + 0.473 × height in cm – 48.3.
